Our Mission

Voqi AI helps online coaches and consultants automate their client acquisition using custom‑built AI systems that qualify leads, book appointments, and spark real conversations — 24/7.

Founded by Sterwart Edmond Matafu, a forward‑thinking entrepreneur passionate about automation and business growth, Voqi AI was born from a simple idea: every business deserves a tailored AI solution that actually fits how they operate.

We don’t believe in one‑size‑fits‑all templates. Your brand, your voice, and your process are unique — and your AI should be too. That’s why we build personalized acquisition systems using advanced tools like VAPI and Make.com, integrated seamlessly into your existing workflows.

From AI DM setters that speak your language, to smart appointment forms that qualify leads on autopilot, to voice agents that handle inbound calls with ease — every system we create is crafted around what you need to grow.

You didn’t build your business to spend all day chasing leads. Let AI handle the heavy lifting — so you can focus on what you do best: delivering transformation.
